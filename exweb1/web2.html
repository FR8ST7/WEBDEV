Key Concepts in AI and Robotics

Glossary of Terms

Artificial Intelligence (AI)
The simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions.
Machine Learning (ML)
A subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention.
Robotics
The interdisciplinary branch of engineering and science that deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots.

AI vs. Robotics: Key Differences

Feature Artificial Intelligence (AI) Robotics
Primary Goal Simulate human intelligence and thought processes. Design and build machines capable of physical tasks.
Focus Software, algorithms, data processing, learning. Hardware, mechanics, electronics, movement.
Output Decisions, predictions, insights, generated content. Physical actions, manipulation, navigation.
Example Chess-playing AI, recommendation engines. Assembly line robots, surgical robots.
